Unwilling Chef
User-Centered Design Project
The Unwilling Chef was a project done for my Digital Media minor, in which our team spent the semester planning a large-scale, interactive prototype of an app of our choosing. Along the way, we completed numerous User-Centered Design staples, such as interaction analyses, think-aloud protocols, user surveys, etc.
Uniquely Targeted Meal Planning App
We planned for our app to specifically target those who have trouble meal planning; if they have ADHD or depression, for instance, or are just new to the whole process. This heavily impacted our design, for instance inspiring the inclusion of a interactive "mascot" to take care of by successfully following your meal plan.
